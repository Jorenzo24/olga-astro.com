Natal Chart Reading

Your personal birth chart, read one-to-one and delivered online — the clearest place to begin with astrology.

A natal chart reading (also called a birth chart reading) is calculated from your exact date, time and place of birth. It maps the position of the Sun, Moon and planets at the moment you arrived, and turns that map into a portrait of who you are: your character, your natural talents, the challenges that shape you, and the deeper purpose written into your chart. It is the foundation every other reading builds on.

The foundation reading

What a natal chart reading actually tells you

Think of your birth chart as a snapshot of the sky, taken from your exact place of birth at the exact minute you were born. No two charts are the same — even twins, born minutes apart, carry subtly different maps. A natal chart reading is the work of interpreting that map: translating the Sun, Moon, rising sign, planets, houses and the aspects between them into language you can actually use.

Where a daily horoscope speaks to millions of people at once, a personal natal chart reading speaks only to you. It is not about prediction or fate. It is about self-understanding — seeing your strengths clearly, recognising the patterns that keep repeating, and making your next decisions a little more consciously, and a little more your own.

Most people come to their first birth chart reading with a quiet question underneath the obvious one: why do I keep doing this? or what am I actually here for? A natal chart reading won't hand you a single tidy answer, but it will give you a far clearer map of the territory — and that, for most people, is exactly what changes things.

What your chart reveals

Six things your birth chart can show you

One reading, interpreted as a whole — because the parts of a chart only make sense in relation to each other.

Who you are at the core

Your Sun, Moon and rising sign — the blend behind how you think, what you feel, and how you first come across to others.

Your natural talents

The gifts that come easily to you — often the ones you overlook precisely because they feel effortless — and how to lean into them.

Your challenges & growth

The friction points and recurring patterns in your chart — named honestly, and framed as edges to grow along rather than flaws to fix.

How you love & relate

What you need in relationships, how you give and receive affection, and the dynamics you tend to repeat with the people closest to you.

Your purpose & vocation

The direction your chart leans toward — the kind of work and contribution that tends to feel meaningful rather than merely busy.

Your timing & cycles

The longer rhythms you were born into — the seasons of your life — and how to recognise the one you're moving through now.

Natal chart reading — your questions

The things people most often want to know before booking their first reading.

A natal chart reading, also called a birth chart reading, is a personal one-to-one session in which an astrologer interprets the map of the sky at the exact moment you were born. It describes your character, your natural talents, your challenges and the deeper purpose written into your chart — guidance calculated for you alone, never a generic horoscope.

A daily horoscope is written for everyone born under the same Sun sign — millions of people at once. A natal chart reading is built only from your exact date, time and place of birth, so it reflects the unique combination of Sun, Moon, rising sign, planets and houses that belong to you. The result is specific, personal and far more useful.

Three things: your date of birth, your time of birth (as exact as possible) and your place of birth. With these, Olga calculates your natal chart precisely before your session. Everything you share is treated as private and confidential.

An approximate time still allows a meaningful reading of your Sun, Moon and planets. If you need the full accuracy that the houses and rising sign require, Olga offers birth time rectification, which reconstructs an unknown birth time from real life events.

Your natal chart reading is held online as a personal one-to-one session, wherever you are in the world, and you're welcome to ask questions throughout. Sessions are unhurried and your reading is yours to keep and revisit. Get in touch and Olga will confirm the format and timing that suit you.
