Synastry & Compatibility Reading

Two birth charts read side by side: how you truly meet, where you flow, and where you grow.

A synastry reading (also called a compatibility reading) compares the birth charts of two people to reveal how they actually relate. It shows where there is natural attraction and ease, and where differences ask for patience and understanding. It is valuable for couples, but just as revealing for business partners, a parent and child, close friends, or any relationship that matters to you.

The relationship reading

What a synastry reading actually tells you

Every person carries a birth chart: the map of the sky at the moment they were born. A synastry reading places two of those maps over each other and studies the conversation between them. Where your Sun meets their Moon, where your Venus touches their Mars, where one chart soothes the other and where it sets off sparks, all of it becomes visible. This is relationship astrology in its most personal form.

A daily horoscope match, the kind that tells you two Sun signs are "made for each other", speaks to millions of people at once and means almost nothing. A compatibility reading speaks only to the two of you. It does not reduce your bond to a score or a verdict. Instead it names, in plain language, what already draws you together and what quietly causes friction, so neither one stays a mystery.

Most people come to a synastry reading with one honest question underneath: why is it so easy with them here, and so hard there? A reading will not promise that a relationship is meant to be or doomed to fail. It will show you the real shape of the connection, and that, for most couples and pairs, is exactly what makes loving each other a little more conscious.

What your two charts reveal

Six things a compatibility reading can show you

One reading, interpreted as a whole, because two charts only make sense in relation to each other.

Your natural attraction

What first drew you together and what keeps the spark alive, the chemistry written into the way your charts meet.

How you communicate

The way you each speak, listen and process, and where your styles meet easily or talk past one another.

Where the friction lives

The recurring tensions and sensitive points, named honestly, so they stop catching you both by surprise.

What each of you needs

How you each give and receive love, attention and security, often in very different languages.

Your shared strengths

What the two of you build well together, the ground where your charts genuinely support one another.

How the bond evolves

The longer arc of your relationship, the seasons it moves through, and how to grow alongside each other rather than apart.
