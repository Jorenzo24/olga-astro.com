Learn more about astrology consultations

A closer look at each type of reading and how an online astrology consultation works.

An online astrology consultation is a personal, one-to-one session where a professional astrologer interprets your unique birth chart to answer your real questions. Unlike a generic daily horoscope written for millions of people, a personal astrology reading is calculated from your exact date, time and place of birth, so the guidance is genuinely about you. Sessions are held online, which means you can book an astrology consultation from anywhere in the world.

Most people come with something specific in mind: a career decision, a relationship, a move abroad, or simply a wish to understand themselves more clearly. Others prefer a broad astrology reading online that maps the whole chart. Either way, the aim is the same, practical, grounded insight you can actually use.

Understanding who you are

A natal chart reading (also called a birth chart reading) is the foundation of personal astrology. It looks at the position of the Sun, Moon and planets at the moment you were born to describe your character, your natural talents, your challenges and your deeper life purpose. It's the ideal first consultation if you've never had a personal astrology reading before.

Mapping your year ahead

An astrology forecast reading turns to timing. By following the current movement of the planets against your birth chart, a forecast reading highlights the themes, opportunities and pressures of the year ahead, so you can plan launches, changes and big decisions with the cycles rather than against them.

Understanding a relationship

A synastry reading, also known as a compatibility reading, compares two birth charts to show how two people relate: where there's natural attraction and ease, and where effort and understanding are needed. It's valuable for couples, but also for business partners, parents and children, or any important relationship.

Finding the place that fits you

A relocation astrology reading, sometimes called astrocartography, explores how different places on the map activate different parts of your chart. If you're weighing a move or emigration, it can show where you may find better support for love, career, creativity or peace of mind.

Finding your vocation

A career astrology reading focuses on vocation: your talents, your ideal working style and the professional directions your chart favours. It's especially useful for students choosing a path and for anyone considering a career change.

Beyond the core sessions, Olga offers several specialist readings. A children's astrology reading helps parents understand a child's nature and gifts. A medical astrology reading reflects on constitutional tendencies and wellbeing themes in the chart (always alongside, never instead of, professional medical advice). Birth time rectification reconstructs an unknown birth time from real life events, and a horary astrology reading answers one specific, pressing question from a chart cast for the moment you ask.

To book an astrology consultation, simply choose the reading that fits your question and get in touch through the contact page. You'll share your birth details, we'll agree a time, and your personal reading is prepared and delivered online. If you're not sure which reading you need, just describe your situation and Olga will recommend the best starting point, as a professional astrologer, her work is to meet you where you are.
